Critical Computation Lab Assignment 07 | Autobiographical Game

This is my project for 'Autobiographical Game' project.

This project was about making a game that can represent myself.
I made a face full of bubbles that changes colors of bubbles and can interact with moving your mouse.
'Unusual Patissier' is a game about a bunny who wants to join her friend's party but can't afford to buy her a present.
They have been friends since they were babies, so they know each other really well and love each other a lot.
Their friendship is still ongoing, but it has been so long since they've met each other because of their schoolwork and other things.
Their lives have been so busy, as we all do when we grow up.

This game is about what the true friendship is.
Even though the bunny didn't buy the friend a gift, the friend still feels happy because she could see her love in the cake by seeing lots of carrots in the cake, which is her favorite vegetable.
When we feel other people's love, it's not about the money they spend for you, the important thing is how much they care about you.
This is what I feel about friendship and what I always feel about other's gifts.

Below is the game 'Unusual Patissier'. Hope you'll enjoy it!
